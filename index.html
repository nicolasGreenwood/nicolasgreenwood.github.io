Welcome.

I’m an 18 year old aspiring multidisciplinary artist based in Novi Sad, Serbia. My work includes, but is not exclusive to, poetry, prose, design, programming and visual art.

I enjoy the process of capturing an authentic thought or feeling and expressing it through an artistic medium. I believe in art as the ultimate expression of sincerity.

To find out more about me, visit my about page.
